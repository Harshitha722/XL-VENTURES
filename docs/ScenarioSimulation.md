# Scenario Simulation

Scenario simulation is domain-aware and driven by ontology templates, reasoning output, and memory.

Each scenario includes assumptions, actions, expected outcomes, risks, success metrics, confidence, and rank. Examples include healthcare diagnostics and insurance escalation, finance risk mitigation and fraud containment, retail promotions and inventory shifts, manufacturing quality containment, and customer success renewal protection.

The simulator generates three to five ranked scenarios when ontology templates are available and falls back to evidence completion, risk mitigation, and opportunity acceleration for newly synthesized domains.
