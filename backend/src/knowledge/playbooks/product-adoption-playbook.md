TITLE: Product Adoption Playbook

PURPOSE

Help customers improve product usage and increase feature adoption.

WHEN TO USE

- Low login frequency.
- Low feature usage.
- Customer says onboarding is difficult.
- Users are inactive.

INDICATORS

- Login frequency decreased.
- Less than 50% feature adoption.
- New users not onboarded.
- Support tickets related to onboarding.

RECOMMENDED ACTIONS

1. Conduct onboarding session.
2. Schedule product walkthrough.
3. Share product documentation.
4. Assign Customer Success Manager.
5. Track adoption every week.
6. Review product analytics.
7. Schedule follow-up after 14 days.

SUCCESS METRICS

- Active users increased.
- Feature adoption improved.
- Customer satisfaction increased.
