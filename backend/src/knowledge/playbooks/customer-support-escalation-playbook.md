TITLE: Customer Support Escalation Playbook

PURPOSE

Handle customers experiencing support-related issues.

WHEN TO USE

- Multiple unresolved tickets.
- SLA violations.
- Customer dissatisfaction.
- Critical production issue.

INDICATORS

- Priority 1 ticket.
- Support response delay.
- Escalation request.
- Negative feedback.

RECOMMENDED ACTIONS

1. Escalate to Support Manager.
2. Notify Engineering Team.
3. Provide workaround.
4. Schedule customer update call.
5. Monitor issue until resolution.
6. Share RCA after resolution.

SUCCESS METRICS

- Issue resolved.
- Customer informed.
- SLA maintained.
