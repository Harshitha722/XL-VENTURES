TITLE: Customer Renewal Playbook

PURPOSE

This playbook provides Customer Success Managers with a structured process for handling customers whose contracts are approaching renewal.

WHEN TO USE

- Renewal date is within 30 days.
- Customer health score is below 70.
- Customer has raised multiple concerns.
- Product adoption is low.
- Executive stakeholders are not engaged.

RISK INDICATORS

- Login frequency has decreased by more than 30%.
- Feature adoption below 50%.
- Multiple unresolved support tickets.
- Negative meeting sentiment.
- Customer mentions competitors.

RECOMMENDED ACTIONS

1. Schedule an Executive Business Review.
2. Review customer business objectives.
3. Conduct a Product Adoption Workshop.
4. Identify executive sponsor.
5. Discuss renewal timeline.
6. Review open support issues.
7. Create customer success action plan.
8. Follow up every week until renewal.

SUCCESS METRICS

- Renewal completed.
- Increased customer health score.
- Executive stakeholder engagement.
- Higher product adoption.
