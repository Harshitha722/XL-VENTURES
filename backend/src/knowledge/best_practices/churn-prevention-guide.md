TITLE: Churn Prevention Guide

PURPOSE

Reduce customer churn by identifying early warning signals.

EARLY WARNING SIGNALS

- Usage declined more than 40%.
- Renewal within 30 days.
- Low NPS.
- Multiple complaints.
- Executive disengagement.
- Competitor mentioned.

RECOMMENDED ACTIONS

1. Executive Business Review.
2. Product Adoption Workshop.
3. Customer Success Plan.
4. Weekly Follow-up.
5. Renewal Discussion.
6. Escalate high-value accounts.

SUCCESS METRICS

- Churn prevented.
- Customer health improved.
- Renewal completed.
