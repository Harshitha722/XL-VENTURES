TITLE: Customer Success Best Practices

Always monitor customer health weekly.

Review product usage regularly.

Track feature adoption.

Start renewal discussions at least 30 days before contract expiration.

Conduct quarterly Executive Business Reviews.

Document customer feedback after every meeting.

Follow up within 24 hours after critical customer interactions.

Share product documentation whenever new features are released.

Review open support tickets weekly.

Identify upsell opportunities after successful adoption.

Measure NPS quarterly.

Maintain complete CRM notes after every customer interaction.

Escalate high-value accounts immediately when churn signals appear.

Keep executive stakeholders informed.

Continuously update customer success plans.
